# AI & the Australian Economy — A 2027–2030 Forecast

This is my interactive web essay for ECON1626 at RMIT University. The project looks at how artificial intelligence is likely to reshape Australia's economy over the next few years — and more importantly, what needs to happen for the gains to be shared broadly rather than concentrated at the top.

**Live site:** [View on GitHub Pages](https://anannyasaji-oss.github.io/econ1626-ai-future-forecast/forecast.html)

---

## What's the argument?

Australia could capture up to $142 billion annually from AI by 2030 — but that number only means something if the right policy decisions are made between now and 2028. This essay maps out what those decisions are, who needs to make them, and what happens if they don't.

---

## How to read the essay

The whole thing lives in one scrollable webpage (`forecast.html`). Here's what you'll find as you scroll through:

| Section | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Thesis** | The core argument and three key stats that frame everything |
| **Economic Channels** | How AI actually connects to economic outcomes — four mechanisms with an interactive bar chart |
| **Productivity Evidence** | A line chart showing three possible productivity trajectories — you can toggle between them |
| **Scenarios** | Three futures for Australia by 2030 — click between tabs to explore each one |
| **Interactive Simulation** | Sliders that let you build your own scenario and see what the numbers say |
| **Policy Playbook** | What needs to happen, who needs to do it, and what gets traded off |
| **Uncertainty** | An honest look at what this forecast gets wrong and where the gaps are |
| **Methods & Data** | Where the numbers came from and how the essay was put together |
| **References** | All sources with links |

---

## Running it locally

No setup needed at all — just download `forecast.html` and open it in any browser. Everything (charts, fonts, styling) loads automatically. There's nothing to install.

If you want the full repo:
1. `git clone https://github.com/anannyasaji-oss/econ1626-ai-future-forecast`
2. Open `forecast.html` in your browser
